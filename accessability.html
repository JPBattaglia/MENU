Accessibility

We aim to make this website usable for everyone. If anything blocks access (keyboard navigation, screen reader labeling, contrast, form errors, or media controls), tell us and we’ll fix it fast.

Request accessibility help View Services

Note: a widget alone is not “compliance.” Real accessibility comes from auditing and fixing the underlying site. We offer both.

Two ways to implement accessibility support

Choose the approach that matches your budget + risk tolerance.

Menu-Made Accessibility Toolkit

Our site-wide accessibility widget (user controls) plus a practical remediation plan. We fix what matters: labels, focus order, contrast, keyboard navigation, and form errors.

Best for owners who want credible improvements and measurable usability outcomes.

accessiBe Integration (optional)

Prefer a third-party overlay? We can install and configure accessiBe for your site and connect it to your accessibility statement page.

We still recommend baseline remediation work to reduce friction and edge-case failures.

What we fix (the real work)

Semantic structure, headings, alt text, interactive controls, focus management, color contrast, and predictable navigation—so the site works with keyboards and assistive tech.

Report an accessibility issue
